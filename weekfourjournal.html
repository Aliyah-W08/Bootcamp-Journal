Frontend

Coming up to completeing one month of the bootcamp and we are introduced to more difficult parts of JavaScript. We started the week of learning about the 2 types of execution contexts, global and local. Focusing more on the global we saw different examples of code and had to figure out the order of the call stack to understand the execution of code. Staying on the frontend side we looked at the difference between global,local and block scopes and higher order functions. We first learnt the syntax and then how to include functions in functions. OOP (object orirented programming) was next and we were taught how to do it using the concept of classes, subclasses and objects. We created properties, added different instances and chained the methods together at the end to get the full result.
