Deeper into Javascript: JSDOM

As we kicked off the week we dived straight back into JavaScript, we got introduced to JSDOM and learned its different methods. We put our learning into practice by building projects like a drum kit and a keycode generator. These projects allowed us to implement our knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, bringing everything together for hands-on experience. In addition to our frontend work, we also explored backend development. We studied different database types like many-to-many relationships and learned their pros and cons. To tackle these challenges, we focused on more efficient models like the one-to-many setup. We also covered normalization from 1NF to 3NF, using primary and foreign keys to connect data.
